# Writing Help Skill

Use this skill when the user asks for help with writing tasks such as:

- Proofreading text for grammar and spelling
- Editing for clarity and flow  
- Improving writing style
- Suggesting better word choices
- Restructuring sentences or paragraphs

## Guidelines

1. Focus on clarity and readability
2. Maintain the original voice and tone
3. Provide specific suggestions with explanations
4. Point out both content and structural improvements

## Process

When helping with writing:
1. Read through the entire text first
2. Identify major issues (structure, clarity, flow)
3. Address grammar, spelling, and punctuation
4. Suggest improvements for style and word choice
5. Provide overall feedback and recommendations
